Apples Juice

"Ripe apples meet a blender's whirl, Pure essence of orchard, unfurl. Sip the sweetness of nature's pearl."

Ingredients:

  • 3 Oranges
  • 2 Carrots

Instructions:

  1. Peel and slice the oranges.
  2. Wash and chop the carrots.
  3. Run the oranges and carrots through a juicer.
  4. Enjoy your fresh juice!
